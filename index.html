Cruise Me, Cruise Me Babe

Have you ever felt dizzy while looking at the sky, feeling like all this blueness was just a collage? This feeling is the genesis of our project. Inspired by Magritte's paintings, the film tells the story of an insomniac photographer, Maxy, who, during a late-night phone conversation with a mysterious girl named Bliss, recalls witnessing a surreal encounter: the soul of a man dressed in a tuxedo, floating in the sky, being guided through Luxembourg's city streets by an angel.

Because a film lives only when it's seen, help us take it where it's never been!

About the Directors

Alexis Vandeweerd and Jules Wax have been working together since 2016. Together, they have carried out several projects at the frontier of audiovisual research-creation. They both hold accreditation from the Chambre des metiers to work as videographers. Alexis has a degree in Cinematography from IAD Brussels, while Jules is self-taught.
